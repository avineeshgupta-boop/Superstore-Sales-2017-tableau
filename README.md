## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive sales dashboard built using **Tableau** to analyze the performance of a retail superstore dataset (2017).

The dashboard helps uncover key business insights related to sales, profit, and customer segments across different regions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit trends
* Identify top-performing categories and sub-categories
* Evaluate regional performance
* Understand customer segment contributions
* Detect loss-making areas for business improvement

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* 📍 The **West region** generated the highest sales
* 📉 Certain sub-categories like *Tables* showed negative profit
* 🏆 Technology category performed best in terms of profit
* 👥 Consumer segment contributed the most to total sales
* 📦 Office Supplies had consistent but moderate performance

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* Interactive filters (Region, Category, Segment)
* Time-series analysis of sales
* Profit vs Sales comparison
* Category-wise performance breakdown
* Clean and user-friendly layout
